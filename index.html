Our Story

As a proud Marathi family living in St. Louis, we understand the longing for authentic flavors from home.

After years of searching for genuine Marathi ingredients and often coming up empty-handed, we decided to bridge that gap for our community.


Our journey started with a simple mission: to bring the authentic taste of Maharashtra to families like ours in Missouri.

We work directly with trusted suppliers in Maharashtra to ensure every spice, every snack, and every specialty item meets the highest standards of quality and authenticity.


Today, we're proud to serve the growing Indian community in St. Louis with products that aren't just ingredients – they're memories, traditions, and connections to our beautiful heritage.
